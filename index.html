Let me introduce myself

Hi here! I'm a Front End developer from Kyiv. I have a big desire to grow in a team of great people who work on the best result and enjoy the process. Always loved to be actively included. I want to create beautiful websites and applications.

I have a master degree in electromechanics (NTUU 'KPI', Kyiv). My commercial experience as project manager and design engineer tought me to collaborate effectively, communications with international customer, deadlines responsibility, people management and continuous growth.

I have finished Front End offline courses, online courses orginised by EPAM, English school with Advanced level, made pet projects, I'm a constant visitor of Udemy, youtube lessons, documentation etc.

Took part in website creation in a team of two developers. Worked in outsource team as a Front End intern, where I developed landing pages, made bug fixes in React project, was a manual QA.

I'm passioned in languages, I like to improve my English, so I worked as an English tutor. I love to read books, write poems. My sport hobby is squash and riding a bicycle.
